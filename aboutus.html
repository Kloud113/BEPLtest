The Visionaries

Three Generations of Excellence.

Our story begins with a visionary. In 1973, Late Shri Laxmandas C. Bhatia recognized the untapped potential of India's human capital. With unwavering dedication, he established a professional conduit for overseas recruitment, laying the foundation for a global enterprise. Guided by the blessings of our Guru, Shri Sathya Sai Baba of Puttaparthi, his ethos of service and integrity continues to be our true north.

Carrying the mantle forward, Mr. M. L. Bhatia brings over 35 years of unparalleled expertise in manpower consultancy. His strategic acumen has driven the timely, high-stakes deployment of elite recruits across diverse verticals—from Oil & Gas and Aviation to Healthcare and Civil Engineering. Under his leadership, our footprint has expanded robustly across the Middle East, South East Asia, Europe, and Africa.
